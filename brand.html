HATAKU

F&B Business

As my first business, I chose the F&B business,
I created a brand called Hataku, hataku itself
is taken from Hako ni haitta hottosunakku which means warm snacks in a box.
My brand focuses on selling Japanese-style Dimsum Mentai
using high-quality ingredients and made with care
to create a delicious taste and suitable as a daily snack.

IS THIS SNACK

HEALTHY ?

Of course, in the cooking process alone is free without oil
because it is not fried, dimsum made from 90% quality chicken and shrimp
is steamed until cooked so that it is healthy to consume every day,
contains high protein and low fat.
Mentai sauce is made from Japanese mayonnaise and tobiko
which contains Vitamin B12 and Vitamin D which are good for bone health.

MY NEXT PLANT

For the time being I will continue to focus on developing the Hataku Brand, because I see quite good potential in this F&B sector.
Of course, next time I will try to create a new Brand.
